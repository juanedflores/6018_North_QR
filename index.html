We are also a contemporary art space that houses contemporary art on all of its floors. Our current shows focus on sustainability inside and outside. The inside traces Chicago’s history through this lens and the house itself serves as a model for sustainability (and outside as water saver).

 

Here are the wall labels from our exhibition Myth of the Organic City which explores Chicago and sustainability. Myth of the Organic City wall labels exhibition is throughout the interior of the 3 floors and basement.

 

Our exterior show Circular Flow is a partner project for the Chicago Architectural Biennale. Outside the house, in the front and back yard, it consists of a circular wheel chair ramp (in the back yard) and sustainable circular water saving elements in the front and back yard, in the alley, and on the roofs!
